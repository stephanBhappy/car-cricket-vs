Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  App: Tarmac20

Tarmac20 is a road trip car-spotting game. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

Tarmac20 does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. No accounts, no sign-in, and no data is sent to any server operated by us.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app saves game state (player names, scores, and current screen) to your device's local storage so you can resume a game after closing the app. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you clear your browser or app data.

Third-Party Services

The About section of the app includes an optional Bitcoin donation button provided by Blink. This button opens an external page operated by Blink. If you choose to use it, Blink's own privacy policy applies. Tarmac20 does not receive or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

Tarmac20 does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no advertising, no in-app purchases, and no data collection of any kind.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this policy, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at info@tarmac20.com.
